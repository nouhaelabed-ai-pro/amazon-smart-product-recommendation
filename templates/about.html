About Smart Product Recommender

Our Mission

To provide intelligent product recommendations powered by advanced machine learning algorithms, helping users discover the perfect products that match their needs and preferences.

About Our Dataset

Our recommendation system is powered by a comprehensive dataset of over 1,000 Amazon products, including detailed information about:

  • Product names, categories, and descriptions
  • Pricing information (actual and discounted prices)
  • Discount percentages
  • Product ratings and review counts
  • User reviews and feedback
  • Product images and links
